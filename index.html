About Us

Radon's Pharma Vista is a 9 acre layout with resedential plotting in the prime location of kadthal .The venture is DTCP(Director of Town and Country planning) approved layout.The Open Villa Plots has world class amenities and features.

Pharma vista has road facing of 1200 feet in existing kadthal to mucherla connecting road. It is the best location for those who are willing to stay and ready for construction in a peaceful environment. You and your family can get the perfect entertainment and can easily set the tone for calm and relaxing home.

We pride ourselves on delivering high quality Open Villa Plots in safe and secure locations which are affordable yet full of luxurious and modern amenities. Paved areas form ideal spots for outdoor furniture and paths for foot traffic Garden beds and plants offering privacy and soften finishes Grassed areas create play areas and free-roaming ground especially if you have kids and animals.

The swimming pool is the Spotlight in the green avenue plantation venture Clubhouse Community tending to gather for group activities, social support, public information and other purposes Attractive playground equipment offering kids fresh air, fun and exercise.
